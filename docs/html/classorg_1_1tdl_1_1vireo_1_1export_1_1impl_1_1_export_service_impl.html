Generate an export.

Exports may take a substantial amount of time, and they are not stored on the server. So it is imperative that the caller handle the ChunkStream and send it to the user's browser.

Parameters
packagerThe packager format.
filterThe filter to select submissions.
Returns
A stream of the export.
